About

Hi, I'm Ramil.

I'm a photographer based in George Town, Grand Cayman. Caymanian by home, Filipino by heart. For years I've made my living chasing the island's best light and the people standing in it.

My work lives somewhere between documentary and a love letter: real moments, warm tones, nothing stiff. Whether it's the morning of a wedding, a growing family, or your first trip to the island, my job is to make pictures you'll still want on the wall in twenty years.
